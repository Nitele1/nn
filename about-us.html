WHO ARE WE

Nitele is a company founded in 2018 that specializes in providing industrial automation parts. Our product range includes PLC systems, HMI monitors, motors and encoders, drives, CNC machines, and general automation. Our customers include equipment manufacturers, automation system integrators, smart manufacturing factories, distributors, intermediaries, and repairers from all over the world. We offer products from leading brands such as Allen-Bradley, Siemens, ABB, Eaton, Sick, Schneider Electric, Bosch, Pro-face, Omron and more.

At Nitele, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality products and exceptional customer service. Our team of experts has extensive knowledge and experience in the field of industrial automation and is always ready to help our customers find the best solutions for their needs. We strive to build long-term relationships with our customers by providing them with reliable and cost-effective solutions.

In addition to our wide range of products, we also offer custom solutions to meet the specific needs of our customers. Whether you need a single part or a complete automation system, we have the expertise and resources to help you achieve your goals. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help your business grow.
